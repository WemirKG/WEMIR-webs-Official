PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy at WEMIR webs

We know you’re trusting us with your information when you reach out about a website. This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Last updated: November 30, 2025

Who we are

WEMIR webs is a small, AI-focused web studio that designs and builds websites and related digital experiences. When this policy says “we,” “us,” or “our,” it’s talking about the WEMIR webs team.

What this policy covers

This policy applies to information we collect:

  • On this website (including all WEMIR webs pages)
  • Through forms you submit, like the contact form or intake form
  • Through email or messages you send us directly

It does not cover information collected on other websites we link to, or information you share with third-party tools we don’t control.

Information we collect

We collect two main types of information:

1. Information you choose to share

  • Name, email, and contact details
  • Business or brand name
  • Social handles you share (like Instagram)
  • Project details, website goals, and preferences you describe in forms or messages

2. Technical & usage information

Like most websites, some technical data may be collected automatically when you visit, such as:

  • Basic device and browser information
  • Approximate location based on IP (at a general level)
  • Pages visited and actions taken on the site

This type of information is generally used in an aggregated, non-personal way to understand how people are using the site.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your questions and project inquiries
  • Prepare quotes, site structures, and suggestions for your website
  • Send follow-up emails about your project or intake form
  • Improve how the site looks, feels, and performs
  • Protect the site from spam and misuse

We do not use your information to do shady data selling or random mass marketing.

When we share information

We keep your information as private as possible and only share it in limited situations, such as:

  • Service providers: For example, form tools (like Formspree/Freespree), email services, hosting providers, or basic analytics platforms that help us run the site.
  • Legal reasons: If we are required to do so by law, or to protect our rights, security, or the rights and safety of others.

We don’t sell your personal information to third parties for their own marketing lists.

Cookies & tracking

This site may use cookies or similar technologies to keep things running smoothly and to understand how people move through the pages.

These can be used to:

  • Help the site function correctly
  • Remember basic preferences (where applicable)
  • Gather anonymous usage stats (like page views)

You can usually adjust cookie settings in your browser if you prefer to limit or block them. Some features may not work as smoothly with cookies disabled.

How long we keep your information

We keep your information only for as long as it makes sense for:

  • Responding to your questions or intake
  • Planning, building, or maintaining your website
  • Keeping basic business records and respecting legal requirements

When that information is no longer needed, we aim to delete it or anonymize it within a reasonable time.

Your choices & rights

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights over your personal information, such as:

  • Seeing what information we have about you
  • Asking us to update or correct it
  • Asking us to delete certain information
  • Asking us not to use your info for optional messages or follow-ups

If you’d like to request any of these, email us using the contact details at the bottom of this page. We may need to confirm your identity before acting on the request.

Children’s privacy

WEMIR webs is not designed for children under 13, and we don’t knowingly collect personal information from children in that age group. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Links to other websites

Our site may link out to other websites, tools, or platforms (for example, examples of sites we’ve built or tools we recommend). We’re not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites, so we encourage you to review their policies if you share information with them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the site, tools, or laws change. When we do, we’ll update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. If the changes are significant, we may highlight them more clearly on the site.
